Marwadi University

NAAC A+

 

Marwadi University (MU), located in Rajkot, Gujarat, is a premier NAAC A+ accredited institution established in 2016. It offers diverse UG, PG, and PhD programs in engineering, management, science, law, and pharmacy. Known for its strong industry-linked curriculum, global alliances and patents, it is a top choice for technical education and research in Western India.
